While travelling the realm of dreams, I discovered a small planet...

Phantasmagoria is the invention of Tamura Shigeru, an illustrator, mangaka, and animated-film maker from Japan. Inspired by his work—particularly, the series of animated shorts A Piece of Phantasmagoria, and its corresponding point-and-click video game—this is a fansite dedicated Tamura's creation.

The website is still under construction. If you've somehow stumbled across this, then please forgive its current state.
